About codeIt

Welcome to codeIt. A place for web developers to share, engage, and learn. codeIt was developed with one goal in mind, to help bring together our bootcamp class where they can post and comment on ideas, the latest development trends, and information. We hope we can help aid in your progression to becoming a full stack developer. We also hope we can help keep contact with our class as we graduate and move on in our development career and lives.

Share

Anyone can post in one of the subject areas. Members can share content including stories, links, and images.

Discuss

You can comment on any post. Comments often help provide additional information, discussion, context, and humor. We look forward in seeing what you have to add to this site.

Have Fun

Remember we are all human. Our actions affect real people and communities, so do the right thing—even when it’s difficult.

Move forward and grow together. Be optimistic about our future. Know that change is necessary for us to progress.
